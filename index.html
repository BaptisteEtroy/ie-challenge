PhotoAuth

"Bringing Transparency to Social Media"

PhotoAuth is a groundbreaking tool designed to promote authenticity across social media platforms. Integrated with apps like Instagram, our technology detects whether images are edited or photoshopped, encouraging users to share genuine content.

Features


AI-Powered Detection

Our advanced AI algorithms analyze images to determine if they've been edited or manipulated. This ensures that unmarked edited photos are identified, promoting honesty among users.


User Declaration

When uploading a photo, users are prompted to declare whether the image has been edited. This fosters a culture of transparency and trust within the community.


Platform Integration

PhotoAuth seamlessly integrates with multiple social media platforms, making it easy for users and developers to adopt. Our API allows for smooth implementation and consistent user experience.
